Meet N8

My name is Nathaniel, which I like to abbreviate as N8 (Nate). I started my career in Google and lived and travelled in different countries. Throughout the years, I've become passionate about the web and how it works, and so I took courses on Treehouse and Udacity to learn more. In May 2015, I took a step farther and enrolled with Thinkful to become a Front End Web Developer. I've completed the first course and while I'm going to tackle the AngularJS next, I built this website to practice my skills and showcase my portfolio (below), which I will continue to work on.
My projects up until now are created using HTML5, CSS3, Sass, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Ajax.
I currently reside in London, UK, where I'm working as a network analyst, while I continue studying coding and I've started to look for opportunities to put my self-thought skills into practice. In my free time, I like to learn more about Web Development, travelling to see new places (mountains are my sweet spot!), playing console games and dreaming of one day moving to Barcelona with my girlfriend and getting ourselves a pug!

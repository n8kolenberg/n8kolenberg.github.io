Introduction

My name is Nathaniel, which I like to abbreviate as N8 (Nate). I started my career in business analysis at Google, but I noticed that my passion and motivation lay elsewhere. I found that I had a desire to create things that people would enjoy using. I turned to web development and started the Thinkful Front End Development course in May 2015. I've completed this course and while I'm going to tackle the Angular.js course next, I want to showcase my portfolio (in progress) below.

I currently reside in London, UK, where I'm still working as an analyst, until I'm done with these courses and can officially change careers to Front End Web Development. In my free time, I like to study all that's needed to get to that end-goal, playing console games and dreaming of one day moving to Barcelona with my girlfriend and getting ourselves a pug!
